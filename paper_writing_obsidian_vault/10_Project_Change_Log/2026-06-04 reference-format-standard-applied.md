# Change

The manuscript formatting was updated so that subsection headings and body text follow the reference article `Manuscript-R0319-Without Mark.docx`.

# Applied Standard

- Level-1 theory headings use `Heading 1`, Times New Roman, 14 pt, bold, first-line indent 0 pt, no extra paragraph spacing.
- Subsection headings use a reference-style `Heading 2` appearance, Times New Roman, 12 pt, bold, first-line indent 0 pt, 1.5 line spacing, no extra paragraph spacing.
- Body paragraphs use Times New Roman, 12 pt, justified alignment, 11.95 pt first-line indent, 1.5 line spacing, and no extra paragraph spacing before or after.
- Display equations keep Word OMML equation objects and the existing center/right-tab layout for right-aligned equation numbers.
- Prose paragraphs containing inline variables or inline OMML are treated as body text, not as display-equation paragraphs.

# Output

The formatted manuscript was saved as `manuscript_reference_format.docx`.
