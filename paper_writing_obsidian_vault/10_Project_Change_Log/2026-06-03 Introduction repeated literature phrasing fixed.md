# Introduction repeated literature phrasing fixed

## Change

Revised the adjacent Chu and Zhang literature-review sentences in the introduction experimental-literature paragraph.

The earlier wording used nearly the same method pattern twice:

- Chu: microstructure characterization + mechanical property testing
- Zhang: microstructure observation + mechanical property testing

The revised wording merges the two citations into one sentence and uses a shared-method frame:

`Chu 等[22]与 Zhang 等[23]均结合显微组织分析和力学性能评价，分别探讨了...`

## Reason

When two adjacent papers use similar methods, repeating the same sentence template makes the paragraph sound mechanical and weakens academic flow.
