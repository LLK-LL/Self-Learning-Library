# 2026-06-03 Workflow governance separated from paper writing rules

## Change Points

- The user identified a major self-growth network problem: workflow logic was being summarized as if it were a paper-writing requirement.
- A dedicated workflow-governance path was introduced: `35_Workflow_Governance`.
- `30_Writing_Rules` should now store intermediate paper-writing rules only.
- Workflow, automation, memory-management, and knowledge-base maintenance rules should be stored separately and must not constrain manuscript claims, structure, wording, or evaluation criteria.
