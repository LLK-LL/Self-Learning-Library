# Process governance must not become manuscript requirements

## Rule Type

- `process_governance`

## Governance Rule

Workflow, memory-management, automation, and knowledge-base maintenance rules must be kept separate from paper-writing requirements. They may guide how the knowledge base grows, but they must not be applied as claims, structure, wording, or evaluation criteria for the manuscript itself.

## Boundary

- This rule governs the knowledge-base workflow only.
- It must not be treated as a manuscript claim, section-structure requirement, wording rule, or paper evaluation criterion.

## Evidence Memories

- [[KB RAG should retrieve selectively and preserve evidence boundaries]]

## Governance Test

Before promoting any rule, label it as paper-writing or process-governance; only paper-writing rules may constrain manuscript wording, structure, or claims.
