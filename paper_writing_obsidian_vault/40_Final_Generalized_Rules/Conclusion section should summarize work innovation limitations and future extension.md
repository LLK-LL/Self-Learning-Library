# Conclusion section should summarize work innovation limitations and future extension

## Final Generalized Rule

An SCI conclusion should begin with an overall summary and then use itemized conclusions to summarize the main work, innovations, advantages, limitations, applicability, and future extensions.

## Detailed Section Rules

- Summarize the main work, method advantages, and key problems solved.
- State the core innovation and compare its advantages with current methods while identifying possible improvements.
- State remaining limitations, practical applicability, and future extensions of the theory, algorithm, or results.
