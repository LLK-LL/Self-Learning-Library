# Numerical simulation section should connect model parameters to research claims

## Final Generalized Rule

A numerical simulation section should build a computational model from the theory or algorithm and extract parameters that are sufficient to explain or prove the paper's research topic.

## Detailed Section Rules

- Build the numerical calculation model from the theoretical model or algorithm.
- When using in-house or commercial software, make figures and curves clean, readable, and clearly annotated.
- Extract parameters that explain or prove the research topic rather than merely displaying simulation output.
