# Reference Material Markers Require Source-Supported Material Names

## Memory

When a manuscript sentence contains a marker such as `（什么材料？）` beside a cited reference, the correction should be made by checking the corresponding cited source rather than inferring from nearby manuscript context.

For Paul et al. [24] in this manuscript, the source information indicates that the investigated explosive-welded plates used carbon or stainless steels as base plates and Ti, Zr and Ta as flyer plates. The material names should be inserted directly into the Paul et al. sentence.

## Boundary

This is a reference-check and introduction-editing memory. It does not create a general manuscript claim beyond the cited reference.
