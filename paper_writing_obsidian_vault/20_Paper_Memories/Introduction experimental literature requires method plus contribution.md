# Introduction experimental literature requires method plus contribution

When expanding the experimental-literature part of the introduction, each cited author's contribution should state both the method and the research content.

Preferred sentence pattern:

`某某等采用/通过/结合某方法，研究/表征/分析了某对象或某问题。`

This prevents the literature review from becoming a list of topics and keeps each contribution traceable to a concrete research approach.
