## Memory Content

When mentioning numerical methods in the introduction, do not add dimensional qualifiers unless the dimension itself is the point being argued. For example, write `SPH-FEM` instead of `two-dimensional SPH-FEM` or `2D SPH-FEM` in the introduction.

In the abstract, avoid abbreviations as much as possible. Prefer full, readable academic expressions unless a term is unavoidable or already standard for the target journal.
