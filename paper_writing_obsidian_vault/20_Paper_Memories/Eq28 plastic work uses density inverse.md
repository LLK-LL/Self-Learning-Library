# Eq28 Plastic Work Uses Density Inverse

For the SPH plastic-work heat-rate contribution, the density term should be the inverse particle density `ρ_I^{-1}`, not a malformed exponent such as `ρ_I^{I-1}`. The strain-rate term should be written as `ε̇_I^p` when the expression represents plastic work rate.
