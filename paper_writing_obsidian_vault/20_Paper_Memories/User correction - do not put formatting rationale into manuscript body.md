# Memory

The user corrected a generated Word document because the body text contained reasoning about formatting, such as saying that a subscript was numeric and should remain upright. This was not manuscript content and should not appear in article prose.

# Application

For all future manuscript and theoretical formula Word outputs, symbol explanations should state only the meaning of the symbol. Formatting rationale, audit decisions, rule reminders, and self-check language must stay in reports or the knowledge base, not in the manuscript body.

When a symbol is explained in prose, use real Word formatting for the symbol itself but do not describe why that formatting was chosen.
